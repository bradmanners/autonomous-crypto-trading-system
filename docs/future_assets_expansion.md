## Recommended Data Provider APIs

### Stock/Options Data
- **Polygon.io** - Real-time stock/options data ($200-400/mo for real-time)
- **Alpha Vantage** - Free tier available for stocks/forex (limited calls)
- **Alpaca Markets** - Free real-time data for account holders
- **Interactive Brokers API** - Comprehensive, requires account

### Forex & Commodities
- **OANDA API** - Forex and some commodities
- **FOREX.com** - Major and emerging market pairs
- **Interactive Brokers** - Full commodities coverage
- **Commodities-API.com** - Simple commodity price API

### Sentiment & Alternative Data
- **Reddit API** - Sentiment analysis for meme stocks (free, rate limited)
- **Twitter/X API** - Social sentiment (paid tiers for volume)
- **NewsAPI.org** - News aggregation
- **Alternative Data Providers:**
  - Quiver Quantitative (Congress trades, insider activity)
  - Unusual Whales (Options flow)

### Economic Data
- **FRED API** (St. Louis Fed) - Free macroeconomic data
- **EIA API** - Free energy data
- **Census Bureau** - Housing and economic data
- **Trading Economics** - International economic calendars

## Risk Considerations by Asset Class

### Stocks/ETFs
- **Liquidity Risk:** Penny stocks can have wide spreads
- **Overnight Risk:** Gaps on earnings/news
- **Leverage Risk:** 3x ETFs decay in ranging markets

### Forex
- **Emerging Markets:** Political risk, low liquidity
- **Spreads:** Can be wide on exotic pairs
- **Leverage:** Easy to over-leverage

### Commodities
- **Contango/Backwardation:** Futures roll costs
- **Storage Reports:** Weekly volatility spikes
- **Geopolitical:** Sudden price shocks

### Meme Stocks
- **Sentiment Reversal:** Rapid crowd exodus
- **Low Float:** Extreme price swings
- **Manipulation Risk:** Pump and dump schemes
